Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to see results from social media marketing?

While some initial improvements can be seen within the first month, meaningful results typically appear within 3-6 months. This timeframe allows for strategy implementation, audience building, content optimization, and algorithm adaptation. We provide monthly performance reports to track progress.

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

The ideal platforms depend on your target audience, industry, and business goals. During our discovery phase, we analyze these factors to recommend the most effective platforms. Common combinations include Facebook and Instagram for B2C, LinkedIn for B2B, and TikTok for younger demographics.

Do you create all the content, or do we need to provide assets?

We handle the complete content creation process, including strategy, copywriting, design, and scheduling. However, we may request specific brand assets, product images, or information to ensure authenticity. For businesses with existing content, we can incorporate and optimize those assets.

How do you measure the success of social media campaigns?

We track a comprehensive set of KPIs aligned with your business objectives. These include engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares), reach and impressions, website traffic, lead generation, conversion rates, and ROI. We provide detailed monthly reports with insights and recommendations for optimization.

Can we adjust our strategy during the contract period?

Absolutely! Social media is dynamic, and we regularly review and adjust strategies based on performance data, algorithm changes, and evolving business goals. We schedule monthly strategy calls to discuss results and make necessary adjustments to ensure we're always working toward your objectives.
